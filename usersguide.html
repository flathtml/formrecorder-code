USER'S GUIDE : How to use the SimpleRecorder

1 : Click on RECORD button to start recording.

2 : Click on STOP button when you are done recording.

3 : The audio player will be displayed wherein you can listen to your recording.

4 : To share your audio file, you will need to click on SHARE button.It will create a shareable link that you can share with others.

5 : To download the audio file to your device you can click on the Save to Device link.

6 : You can also create multiple recordings and choose the best one to share.

PLEASE NOTE : Currently this app does not work in Facebook browser. In case you have opened this via Facebook then you can tap on the three dots at the top right section and a popup window will open up where in you can choose to open it in Chrome. For iphone users, you can open in safari.
