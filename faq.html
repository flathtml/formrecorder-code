Question : How does the customized Google audio form work?

  1. The Form has two parts : (a) Simple Audio recorder and (b) JavaScript Form.
  2. Once the User records the audio using the Simple Audio recorder and hits share button, then a shareable audio link is created and populated in the Form.
  3. The Student can fill the rest of the form and hit SUBMIT button.
  4. The form has a Google Sheet Web API. Thus once the Data is submitted, it is fed to the Google sheet that is linked to the Google Sheet Web API? This Google sheet will be residing in teacher's Google Drive.
  5. A teacher can access all their students recordings via one Google sheet.

Please check the demo form and feel free to record a test audio, then complete the Form details and submit it . The demo Google sheet link will appear at the bottom of the form and you can click on that and view your recording details.
